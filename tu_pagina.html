Academy of Glam is South Florida's Premier Makeup Program

We've added another dimension to make-up education.

Academy of Glam is a one-of-a-kind makeup artistry power program. At Academy of Glam, you’ll train alongside renowned celebrity makeup artists, YouTube makeup tutorial stars, and Fortune 500 beauty business executives to master state-of-the-art techniques, develop a world-class makeup artistry portfolio, and cultivate your true potential as a makeup artist.

Academy of Glam is not a vocational school… we are a fertile breeding ground where you can explore and enhance your passion and skill as a makeup artist whether you are a working artist or simply natural-born makeup geek!

Academy of Glam is not a school of beauty. We are a Beauty Movement!

Our fun, hands-on tutorials are non-traditional; we’ve thrown out the books and usual lesson plans. In fact, our most important tools are not our brushes, it is ourselves. We believe that beauty starts within and radiates out. We believe you can only learn the dance steps so long before you need to get out and dance!

We believe that a great artist makes a great makeup artist; therefore we begin your training with the basic principles of fine art. We blend life coaching into all of our lesson plans so our students have a clear sense of self, direction and purpose. Our photo shoots are purposefully designed to simulate the challenges of real on set environments so you can experience a variety of real industry situations.

Our goal is to provide as true to life experience as possible. Academy of Glam is a playground for creative minds, and inspired makeup artists.

Power Professional Master Program™

Our Signature 18-Week Makeup Course

Whether your desire is to simply turn the beauty you love into what you do, learn techniques to help you launch a new career or simply build a star following on social media, this power program will be your game changer in the world of beauty.

AOG’s Power Professional Master Makeup Artistry Program™ is a full spectrum professional makeup artistry education like no other that also includes empowering life and business development coaching, diverse professional portfolio building and a vortex of real time, real world experiences.

In this one-of-a-kind 18 week program, you’ll train alongside renowned celebrity artists, a top Fortune 500 beauty executive and You Tube tutorial makeup stars to master state-of-the-art techniques, develop a world-class portfolio and cultivate your true potential.

  • 8 Professional Photo Shoots
  • Models Provided for 6 Photo Shoots
  • 8 Edited, Digital Photos For Your Portfolio
  • Pro Supplies Provided During Special FX, Henna and Face Painting
  • One-on-One Mentor Meetings With Lead Artists & Industry Executives
  • Lifetime Membership – AOG Artists Network
  • Access Pass – Free Attendance To Future Group Program Classes
  • Power Professional Master Artistry – Certificate of Completion
  • Graduation Ceremony
  • Professional Headshot
